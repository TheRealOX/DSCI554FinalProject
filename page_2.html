Data Source

The data for this map and slider bar chart is from Espn, Hoopshype, and Kaggle.

In this map, we present NBA team level data in different dimensions in 2021-2022 season such as total number of playoff games and total number of NBA championship players in a certain team. We highlight the championship team in 2021-2022 season to figure out what is special about the championship team. In the horizontal bar chart, we present NBA team salary data from 2010-2022 since we believe a team’s spending on players’ salary determines the team’s performance to some extent..

What We Found

In this map, you can use the dropdown menu to select the data that you want to view. The size of the circle represents the value, and you can hover over to the circle to check the value. Championship team is highlighted in gold color, and all playoff teams are also highlighted in another color. Non-playoff teams are not counted in the data. As we can see, the championship Golden State Warriors ranks high in each data though it does not rank at the first place. Thus, we believe there is a positive correlation between the championship and these presented factors.

Map for NBA Team's Data in 2021-2022 Season Play-offs

What We Found

In this bar chart, we can use the slide bar to change team salary data in different years, and the championships from 2010 to 2022 are listed on the right side. We can discover around half of the championship teams rank at the top of each year’s salary. Thus, we believe that there is a positive correlation between the championship and a team’s spending on salary.
